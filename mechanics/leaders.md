## Skills

| Skill | Description |
|---|---|
| **Assassin** | gives a N% chance of assassinating a random enemy agent each turn. |
| **Commando** | increases the ground combat bonuses of all troops by N, who may either be on a ship or stationed on a planet. |
| **Engineer** | increases the restoration of a ship's shields by N%. In addition, the engineer repairs all damage after battle. |
| **Famous** | increases the chance to recruit other officers and decreases the cost of hiring other officers by N BCs. |
| **Financial Leader** | increases the credit income of a colony by N%. |
| **Helmsman** | increases the defensive bonus of the ship by N. |
| **Instructor** | increases experience points by N per turn for ALL ships. Only the BEST bonus for all instructors are used. |
| **Labor Leader** | increases the productivity of factory workers by N%. |
| **Navigator** | allows all accompanying ships to ignore the movement restrictions of moving ships through a nebulae or a black hole and raises the speed of all ships by N. |
| **Operations** | increases the command rating of ships by N. |
| **Ordnance** | increases the maximum damage of a ship by N. |
| **Researcher** | increases the research points generated by N per turn. |
| **Security** | increases the combat strength of a ship's marines by N when boarded by enemy marines. |
| **Spy Master** | increases the spying bonus of all spies operating in alien empire by N. |
| **Tactics** | increases the initiative of all accompanying ships at the same location by N. |
| **Trader** | increases the monetary bonus of trade agreements by N. |
| **Weaponry** | increases the attack bonus of the ship by N. |
