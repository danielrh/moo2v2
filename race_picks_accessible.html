Usage notes

The explicit Normal choices are an accessibility addition; the original game represented them by leaving a category unselected. The calculator enforces the classic limit of no more than 10 picks gained from disadvantages, prevents spending more picks than are available, and applies the most obvious mutually exclusive trait pairs.

Pick costs follow the supplied screenshot. Fan patches and mods can change costs or effects.
